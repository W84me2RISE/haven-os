# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Haven OS is a project built around the dignity and autonomy of its
users. The same values apply to how people interact within the project
itself.

We expect everyone participating in Haven OS — through issues, pull
requests, discussions, or any other channel — to:

- Engage honestly and in good faith
- Criticize ideas, not people
- Accept that others may disagree, and engage with the disagreement
- Respect that maintainers make final decisions about the project's
  direction

We do not tolerate:

- Harassment of any kind
- Personal attacks
- Deliberate intimidation
- Sustained disruption of discussion

---

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all project spaces — GitHub issues,
pull requests, discussions, and any other venue where Haven OS business
is conducted.

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in comments being removed, contributors being
blocked, or other actions at the discretion of the maintainers.

This is a small project in early stage. The standard is plain: behave
the way you would want others to behave in a space you care about.

---

## Foundation

This project's values are grounded in its charter. The same commitment
to human dignity that shapes the OS shapes this community.

The charter is here:
